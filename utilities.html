PaymentID Generator

Payment ID is optional transaction attachment that is usually used to identify transactions. Because of anonymity of PalladiumCoins transactions, without Payment ID you will not be able to distinguish them. You can generate random Payment IDs and give them to each user who is to send you some PC. This way you can tie payments to users.
